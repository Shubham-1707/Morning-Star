School Uniform

BOYS - SUMMER

1. Shorts ( Classes I to V ) Grey

2. Trousers Classes ( VI to XII ) Grey

3. Shirt White (Half sleeve) with School monogram.

4. Socks White with two maroon Stripes on top

5. Shoes Black


BOYS - WINTER

1. Blazer Maroon with School Crest

2. Jersey Maroon with School Crest.


NOTE

1. A clean handkerchief is compulsory

2. Shoes must be polished everyday

3. Nails should be clean and cut short

4. Hair must be properly cut and should give a tidy appearance.


GIRLS - SUMMER

1. Skirts Classes ( I TO V ) Grey with pleats.

2. Blouse White ( Half Sleeve ) with School monogram

3. Kameez ( Classes VI to XII ) Grey

4. Salwar White

5. Duppatta White Cotton

6. Socks White with two maroon Stripes on top

7. Shoes Black


GIRLS - WINTER

1. Blazer Maroon with School Crest

2. Jersey Maroon with School Crest.


NOTE

1. A clean handkerchief is compulsory.

2. Shoes must be polished everyday.

3. Nails should be clean and cut short.

4. No “Bindi “ make-up or ornaments are permitted.

5. Only short hair may be kept open ; Long hair must be plaited or tied with ribbons


P.T. UNIFORM FOR BOYS

White Shorts /Trousers and shirts.

White Canvas shoes and socks.


P.T. UNIFORM FOR GIRLS

White Divided skirts , Blouses and bloomers. (Classes I to VII)

White Kameez /salwar. ( Classes VIII to XII ) White Canvas shoes and socks.


NOTE

1. Blazers are compulsory for all students. It is mandatory to wear Blazers from 15th Nov 2015 to 15th Feb 2016.

2. Track suits as per the school approved pattern are compulsory for all students on every Wednesday throughout the session.

3. P.T. Uniform (white) is compulsory for all students on every Friday.
