Admission Procedure

Morning Star School follows the standardized admission procedure as recommended for CBSE-Affiliated schools. Documents Required

 

* Previous School Records * Merit Certificates
* Birth Certificate
* Transfer Certificate
* Certificates of Special Achievements
* Two Photograph and other Credentials
As required by the school must be presented at the time of registration.
